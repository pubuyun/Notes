### 1. Opening – Calm Before Change
- Setting: **[Describe the place in detail]**
    - Time of day
    - Weather
    - Sounds / smells
- Protagonist alone, doing something ordinary
- Mood: peaceful / bored / distracted
- Hint that the place feels **empty or forgotten**

---

### 2. Disruption
- An unusual sound / movement interrupts the calm
- Protagonist notices something **out of place**
- Brief hesitation → curiosity wins
- **Describe atmosphere changing**
    - Light
    - Temperature
    - Silence or echoes

---

### 3. Investigation
- Protagonist moves deeper into the setting
- Finds a **strange or hidden object**
- Object feels wrong:
    - too warm / cold
    - old but intact
- **Pause for description of thoughts and fear**

---

### 4. Escalation
- The environment reacts
- Time / space feels unstable or distorted
- Protagonist realises something is happening that **cannot be explained**
- Panic rises
- Attempts to leave fail

---
### 5. Encounter
- Meets another figure / presence
- Initial confusion or **disagreement**
- Short dialogue
- Realisation they are both trapped or affected
---

### 6. Turning Point
- Danger increases (collapse, pursuit, injury, time running out)
- One character is at risk
- Protagonist must make a quick decision
- **Describe physical action and emotion**
---

### 7. Climax
- A decisive moment involving the object / environment
- A rescue, escape, or sacrifice
- Tension peaks
- Setting begins to change again
---

### 8. Resolution / Ending (Open-Ended)
- Calm returns, but something is different
- One character gone / object missing / place abandoned again
- Protagonist reflects briefly
- Ending suggests a **new beginning** or lasting impact

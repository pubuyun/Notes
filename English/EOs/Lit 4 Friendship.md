## **2. Point 1 – Extract (60% weight)

Ruth’s confession reveals guilt + desire to repair three-way friendship**
### **Point**
In the extract, Ishiguro presents the friendship by reconciliation, with Ruth finally admitting how she damaged the bond between the three of them and showed desire to repair the three way friendship.

### **Evidence (Extract)**
“_The main thing is, I kept you and Tommy apart… That was the worst thing I did._”

### **Techniques & Analysis (AO2)**
- **Emotional, fragmented speech** shows Ruth’s sincerity and shame.
- Tommy’s *“child-like curiosity”* highlights his **vulnerability** and the lingering **innocence** in their friendship.
- (“_that was what started me sobbing_”) **Kathy’s first-person narration** heightens the emotional impact—her crying shows years of unspoken pain .

### **Link / Reader**
Shows the trio’s friendship as damaged but meaningful—this is a moment of directly facing their emotions.

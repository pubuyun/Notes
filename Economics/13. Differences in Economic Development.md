## Economic Development
> *Economic Growth* is a quantitative measure
> *Economic Development* combines quantitative and qualitative measures

## Factors
- Political stability
- Macroeconomic stability
- Barriers to trade
- Levels of infrastructure
- Saving rates / foreign aid
- Educational standards
- Natural resources

## Reasons
- Income
- Productivity
- Population growth
- Saving and Investment
- Tertiary Sector

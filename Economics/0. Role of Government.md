
| **Area of Intervention** | **Examples of Intervention**                                                                                                                                                   | **Explanation**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Local**                | - Local health services<br>    <br>- Refuse collection<br>    <br>- Parking fines<br>    <br>- Local prosecutions<br>    <br>- Parks & recreation<br>    <br>- Public services | - Local governments are **responsible for delivering government services** on a town/regional basis<br>    <br>- They usually receive funding from the central government & are **held accountable** for the quality of goods/services provided **by the voters** in their area<br>    <br>- Local government branches are often referred to as **'councils', 'federal' or 'state'** |
| **National**             | - Central Government<br>    <br>- **Fiscal Policy**<br>    <br>- **Monetary Policy**<br>    <br>- **Supply-side Policy**                                                       | - Determining the **best combination of policies** that will help them to meet all of their **macroeconomic aims**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
| **International**        | - Exchange rate interventions<br>    <br>- **Protectionism**                                                                                                                   | - **International trade** is vital to economic growth in many economies<br>    <br>- Governments have a role to both **protect domestic industry** & to help it **compete internationally**                                                                                                                                                                                          |

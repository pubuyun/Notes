## Human Development Index
### Components
#### Gross national income (PPP)
- Average income of a person within a country
- Real National Income / num. people
- How much an individual from each country can purchase given the average amount of income that they have
#### Live expectancy at birth
#### Education
- Mean years of schooling of those aged 25
- Expected years of schooling of those entering school
#### Gender inequality
- Reproductive Health
- Empowerment
- Labour market
**Not**: poverty, political freedom

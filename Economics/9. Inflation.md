## Consumer Prices Index
> compiled by *Office of National Statistics*

based on a basket of goods and services, weighed by proportion of income
### Basket of Goods

| Constants     | 1947                   | Recent                 |
| ------------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Bread         | Sewing Machines        | Broadband Subscription |
| Milk          | Condensed Milk         | Smart phone            |
| Egg           | Mutton                 | Gaming                 |
| Petrol        | Unskinned Wild Rabbits | Music download         |
| School Uni    | Lamp Oil               | E-book                 |
| Gas+Elec bill | Gramophone Records     | Blu-ray Discs          |

$$CPI = \frac{\text{Current Price}}{Base Year}\times 100$$
$$\text{Inflation Rate} = \frac{\text{Ending Index} - \text{Beginning Index}}{\text{Beginning Index}}\times 100\%$$

## Caltech
[Pietro Perona - Division of Engineering and Applied Science](https://www.eas.caltech.edu/people/perona)
- Perona 教授还对研究人类如何执行视觉任务感兴趣，例如搜索和识别图像内容。他最近的一个项目
研究如何利用网络上成千上万的人的视觉能力来众包图像注释。
- In collaboration with his students, he develops algorithms to enable machines to learn to recognize frogs, cars, faces and trees with minimal human supervision, and to enable machines to learn from human experts.

This enables geneticists and neuroethologists to investigate the relationship between genes, brains and behavior.

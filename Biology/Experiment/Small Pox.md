## Pathogen
Caused by *Orthopoxvirus variola*, common name *Variola Virus*
![Pasted image 20250312104945|300](https://pu6uyun-image.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/Pasted%20image%2020250312104945.png)
## Mechanism
Once **inhaled**, the **variola virus** invaded the _mucous membranes_ of the **mouth**, **throat**, and **respiratory tract**.  
    一旦被吸入，天花病毒便侵入口腔、喉咙和呼吸道的黏膜。
In the **initial growth phase**, the virus seemed to **move from cell to cell**, but by around the **12th day**, widespread _lysis_ of infected cells occurred.  
    在最初的生长期，病毒似乎在细胞间传播，但到第 12 天左右，受感染细胞大范围溶解。
At this stage, the virus could be found **in the bloodstream in large numbers**, a condition known as **viremia**.  
    此时，大量病毒进入血液，被称为病毒血症。
This resulted in the **second wave of multiplication** in the **spleen**, **bone marrow**, and **lymph nodes**. 
    这导致病毒在脾脏、骨髓和淋巴结中进行第二轮增殖。

## Symptoms
### **Early Symptoms 早期症状**

- **Fever** of at least **38.3°C (101°F)**, _muscle pain_, _malaise_, _headache_, and _fatigue_.  
    体温至少 38.3°C（101°F），肌肉疼痛、不适、头痛_和_疲劳。
- As the _digestive tract_ was commonly involved, _nausea_, _vomiting_, and _backache_ often occurred.  
    由于消化道常受影响，恶心、呕吐和背痛时常发生。
- The **early prodromal stage** usually lasted **2–4 days**.  
	    早期前驱期通常持续2-4 天。·

### **Late Symptoms 晚期症状**

- By **days 12–15**, the first visible **lesions** – small reddish spots called _enanthem_ – appeared on _mucous membranes_ of the _mouth_, _tongue_, _palate_, and _throat_, while the **temperature fell to near-normal**. These **lesions rapidly enlarged and ruptured**, releasing large amounts of _variola virus_ into the _saliva_.  
    到第 12-15 天，第一可见的病变——称为_皮疹_的小红色斑点——出现在_口腔_、舌头、腭部_和 喉咙 的 粘膜 上，同时体温降至接近正常。这些病变迅速扩大并破裂，释放大量_天花病毒_到_唾液_中。
- _Variola virus_ tended to attack _skin cells_, causing the characteristic **pimples (macules)** associated with the disease.  
    天花病毒_倾向于攻击_皮肤细胞，引发该疾病特有的丘疹（斑疹）。
- A **rash** developed on the skin **24 to 48 hours** after lesions on the **mucous membranes** appeared.  
    皮疹在黏膜病变出现后 24 至 48 小时内发展。
- Typically, the **macules** first appeared on the **forehead**, then **rapidly spread** to the whole **face**, **proximal portions of extremities**, **trunk**, and finally to the **distal portions of extremities**. The **process took no more than 24 to 36 hours**, after which **no new lesions** appeared.  
    通常，斑疹首先出现在前额，然后迅速扩散到整个面部、四肢近端、躯干，最后到四肢远端。这个过程不超过 24 至 36 小时，之后不再出现新的病变。

## Transmission
- **Transmission** occurred through **inhalation** of _airborne_ **variola virus**, usually **droplets** expressed from the _oral_, _nasal_, or _pharyngeal mucosa_ of an infected person.  
    通过吸入空气中的天花病毒传播，通常是由感染者的口腔、鼻腔或咽部粘膜释放的飞沫。
- It was transmitted **primarily through prolonged face-to-face contact** with an infected person.  
    主要通过长时间与感染者面对面接触从一人传播给另一人。

## Treatment/Prevention

**Variolation (Early Inoculation)**  
**人痘接种（早期免疫）**

**Vaccination (Edward Jenner, 1796)**  
**疫苗接种（爱德华·琴纳，1796年）**

**Mass Vaccination Campaigns (20th Century)**  
**大规模疫苗接种（20世纪）**

**Smallpox Eradication (WHO, 1980)**  
**天花根除（世卫组织，1980年）**

- 1977: Last natural smallpox case reported.  
    1977年：最后一例自然天花病例报告。
- 1980: WHO declared smallpox eradicated.  
    1980年：世卫组织宣布天花被根除。

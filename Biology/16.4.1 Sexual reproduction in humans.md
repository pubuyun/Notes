
## Male Reproductive System
![image.png](https://pu6uyun-image.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/20251009144711.png)

### Testes 睾丸
> production of male gametes(sperm)
### Scrotum 阴囊
> carry testes outside the body, keep cooler
### Sperm Ducts 输精管
> a way from testes to urethra
### Prostate Gland 前列腺
> produce semen, which carries sperm
### Urethra 尿道
> Carries sperm and urine out of the body
### Penis 阴茎
> Inserted in to vagina during sexual intercourse

## Female Reproductive System
![image.png](https://pu6uyun-image.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/20251009144705.png)


### Ovary 卵巢
> release ovum(卵子)
### Oviducts 输卵管
> a way from ovary to uterus
> fertilisation occurs here
### Uterus 子宫
> Muscular bag with a soft **lining**
> where fertilized egg will be implanted to develop into a foetus
### Cervix 宫颈
> Ring of muscle
> keep developing foetus in place
### Vagina 阴道
> Muscular tube
> where the males penis enter during sexual intercourse, and sperms are deposited

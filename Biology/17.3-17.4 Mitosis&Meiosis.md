## Mitosis
> nuclear division giving rise to genetically identical cells 

### Stem Cells 干细胞
> unspecialized cells that divide by mitosis to produce daughter cells that can become specialised for specific functions

- Create new tissue to replace damaged organs(regenerative medicine)
- Treat cancers(leukemia) by replacing cells
## Meiosis
> reduction division in which the chromosome number is halved from diploid to haploid resulting in genetically different cells. 
## Compare

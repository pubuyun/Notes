## Evolution
> The change in **adaptive features** of population over time
## Natural Selection
1. genetic variation within populations 
2. production of many offspring 
3. struggle for survival, including competition for resources 
4. a greater chance of reproduction by individuals that are better adapted to the environment than others 
5. these individuals pass on their alleles to the next generation
## Selective Breeding
 1. selection by humans of individuals with desirable features
 2. crossing these individuals to produce the next generation 
 3. selection of offspring showing the desirable features

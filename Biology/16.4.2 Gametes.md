> [!def] fertilisation
> the fusion of the nuclei from a male gamete (sperm) and a female gamete (egg cell)
## Male Gamete
- **Flagellum**: tail, to swim  
- **Middle piece**: contains mitochondria  
- **Head**: streamlined  
- **Cell membrane**  
- **Acrosome**: similar to lysosome, contains digestive enzymes  
- **Nucleus**: contains father's DNA
## Female Gamete
- **Energy stores and jelly coat**: provide energy and protection  
- **Jelly coat**: only lets one sperm in  
- **Cell membrane**  
- **Nucleus**: contains mother's DNA
## DNA
- determine our characteristics
- hereditary
### Haploid cells
> contains half the DNA
### Diploid cells
> contains full amount of DNA

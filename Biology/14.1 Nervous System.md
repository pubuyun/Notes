 > [!def]
> The nervous system **coordinates** and **regulates** body functions
### Central Nervous System
> consists of the *brain* and the *spinal cord*

### Peripheral nervous system
> consists of the peripheral *nerves* that are connected to the **CNS/Spinal cord**

## Nerve
>a bundle of *neurons*
### Neuron
> Specialized cell in the nervous system to carry electrical signals around your body
![image.png|500](https://pu6uyun-image.oss-cn-hongkong.aliyuncs.com/20250428160718.png)

*dendrites*: short fibres of the **cytoplasm**, shorter branched ends
*myelin sheath*: a layer of **fat and protein**, **insulates** the nerve fibres, make transmission **faster**
*axon*: long, extended, thin fibres from the cell body. specialized structure of a neuron to carry electrical impulses faster

### Impulses

## Simple Reflex Arc
### Stimulus
> a change in the environment that you can detect
> detected by **sense organs**
#### Receptors
> group of cells that are sensitive to stimulus

Why different sensitivity:
- different numbers of receptors
- receptors closer together in more sensitive areas/hands, higher density

### Response
#### Effectors
> parts of the body that respond to a stimulus
- Muscle
- Gland

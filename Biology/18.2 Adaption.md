> [!def] adaptation 
> the process, resulting from **natural selection**, by which populations become **more suited to their environment** *over many generations*

> [!def] adaptive feature
> an inherited feature that helps an organism to survive and reproduce in its environment 

> **Hydrophytes**: plants adapted to live and reproduce in wet habitats 
> **Xerophytes**: plants adapted to live and reproduce in dry habitats where water availability is low

| Feature      | Xerophyte          | Hydrophyte                       |
| ------------ | ------------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Waxy cuticle | thick              | thin                             |
| Stomata      | sunken             | concentrated on upper eppidermis |
| Leaves       | small              | flat, large                      |
| Roots        | extensive, shallow | small                            |

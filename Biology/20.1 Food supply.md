## Increase in Food Supply
- **Agricultural Machinery** to use larger areas of land and improve efficiency
- **Chemicals**
	- **Chemical Fertilisers** to improve yields (NO3-)
	- **Insecticides** to improve quality and yield
	- **Herbicides** to reduce competition with weeds
- **Selective Breeding** (selective breed organisms with desired treats through generations) to improve production by crop plants and livestock 

## Monocultures
> An area of round covered by a single crop with almost no other species present
### Pro
- More applicable for machinery
- Large quantity, harvested at once
- Same herbicide and insecticide
### Con
- Reduction in biodiversity
- Reduction in soil fertility
- Volunteer to specific pests since low genetic variation
## Intensive Livestock Production
> Large number of livestock in a single area
### Pro
- Produce more and cheaply
- Take up less land
### Con
- Spread of diseases
- Waste cause water and land pollution
- Require large quantity of water 
- Not energy-efficient

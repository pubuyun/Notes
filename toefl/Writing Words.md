- **首先：** First → **Initially, To begin with, First and foremost**
- **另外：** Also, And → **Furthermore, Moreover, In addition**
- **但是：** But → **However, Nevertheless, On the other hand**
- **所以：** So → **Therefore, Consequently, Thus, As a result**
- **例如：** For example → **For instance, A case in point is that...**
- **总之：** In conclusion → **In summary, To recapitulate**

- `explain` → `elucidate`, `clarify`
- `show` → `demonstrate`, `illustrate`  
- `give` → `provide`, `offer`
- `make sth stronger` → `buttress`, `bolster`

- **提出论点：** `put forward the argument that...`, `is a strong advocate of...`
- **提供证据：** `provide compelling evidence for...`, `lend support to...`
- **削弱观点：** `cast doubt on...`, `undermine the credibility of...`

- **说/认为：** The author **says**... → The author **claims/argues/contends**... (这些词更能体现争论的语境)
- **反驳：** The lecturer **disagrees**... → The lecturer **refutes/challenges/counters** this argument.
- **表明：** This **shows**... → This **demonstrates/illustrates/suggests**...
- **重要的：** important → **crucial, critical, significant, essential**
